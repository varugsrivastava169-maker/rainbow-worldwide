ABOUT RAINBOW WORLDWIDE

Designing Exhibition Experiences That Connect Brands With People

Rainbow Worldwide is a premium exhibition stand design and build company serving ambitious brands that want to make a stronger impact on the show floor. We combine creative strategy, technical planning, production expertise and international project management to deliver exhibition environments that look refined, perform effectively and represent your brand with confidence.

From compact trade show booths to large-scale pavilions, our team manages every stage of the project — concept, design, manufacturing, logistics, installation and final handover — with precision and accountability.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide complete turnkey exhibition stand services?

Yes. We manage concept design, manufacturing, logistics, installation, dismantling and project coordination.

Can you work internationally?

Yes. Rainbow Worldwide supports exhibition projects across Europe and worldwide through experienced project planning and onsite coordination.

Can you design custom exhibition stands?

Yes. We create custom exhibition stands based on your brand, space, objectives, visitor flow and event requirements.

When should we start planning our stand?

Ideally, planning should begin several months before the exhibition to allow enough time for design, approvals, production and logistics.
